The Sanctuary of Truth in Pattaya
  4.4   |  🕘 6 Hours

The Sanctuary of Truth is an architecture marvel composed entirely of wood
without any metal nails. Constructed to preserve the building techniques of the past,
the building exemplifies hand-hewn woodworking skills in Thailand. Built based on
the religious philosophy of impermanence, the complex features four wings dedicated
to Thai, Khmer, Chinese and Indian religious iconography respectively.
